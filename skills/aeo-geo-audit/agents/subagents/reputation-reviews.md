# Reputation and Reviews Agent

Audit trust, review strength, sentiment, and reputation signals that answer engines and customers can verify.

Check:

- Review sources: Google, Yelp, BBB, Trustpilot, industry-specific platforms, marketplace profiles, app stores where relevant.
- Rating, count, recency, velocity, review distribution, and competitor comparison.
- Sentiment themes: service quality, speed, pricing, communication, outcomes, staff, negative patterns.
- Owner responses: speed, specificity, tone, resolution, keyword/entity usefulness without stuffing.
- Website trust signals: testimonials, case studies, third-party logos, certifications, guarantees, policies, real photos, staff bios.
- Review schema and testimonial compliance; flag fake-looking or unverifiable review claims.

Output:

- Reputation summary
- Review gap table
- Sentiment themes
- Response quality assessment
- Trust proof gaps
- Review acquisition and response plan
