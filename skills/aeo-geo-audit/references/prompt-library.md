# Prompt Library

Use these prompts as test patterns. Replace bracketed values with the company, market, service, and competitor context.

## Category Discovery

- "Best [service] companies in [city]"
- "Top-rated [service] provider near [city]"
- "Who are the most trusted [service] companies in [market]?"
- "Which [service] companies serve [neighborhood/county]?"

## Local Recommendation

- "Who should I hire for [problem] near [city]?"
- "Find a local company that can help with [urgent problem] in [city]."
- "What [service provider] has strong reviews near [city]?"
- "Which company is best for [service] in [service area]?"

## Brand Verification

- "Is [company] reputable?"
- "What do reviews say about [company]?"
- "What are common complaints about [company]?"
- "What sources support [company]'s expertise in [service]?"

## Comparison

- "[company] vs [competitor] for [service]"
- "Alternatives to [company] in [city]"
- "Compare [company], [competitor A], and [competitor B]."
- "Which is better for [service]: [company] or [competitor]?"

## Cost and Process

- "How much does [service] cost in [city]?"
- "What is the process for hiring a [service provider]?"
- "How long does [service] usually take?"
- "What questions should I ask before hiring a [service provider]?"

## Problem/Solution

- "How do I solve [problem] for [customer type]?"
- "What causes [problem], and who fixes it near [city]?"
- "What should I do after [event] damages my [asset]?"
- "What are warning signs I need [service]?"

## Capture Fields

Record:

- Date
- Platform/model
- Prompt
- Location context
- Brand mentioned or missing
- Citation/source URLs
- Competitors mentioned
- Accuracy issues
- Likely reason for visibility or absence
