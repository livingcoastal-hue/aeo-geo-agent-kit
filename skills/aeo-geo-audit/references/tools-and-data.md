# Tools and Data Sources

Use what is available in the current environment. Mark unavailable sources as not checked.

## Website Crawl

- Site crawler: Screaming Frog, Sitebulb, Playwright scripts, wget, curl, or browser extraction.
- Structured data: Google Rich Results Test, Schema Markup Validator, local JSON-LD parsers.
- Technical checks: robots.txt, sitemap.xml, HTTP status, canonicals, redirects, title/meta/H1/H2 extraction.

## Local and NAP

- Google Business Profile and Google Maps.
- Bing Places, Apple Business Connect, Yelp, BBB, Facebook, LinkedIn.
- Industry directories and local citation platforms.
- Website footer, contact page, location pages, schema, social profiles.

## Reviews and Reputation

- Google reviews, Yelp, BBB, Trustpilot, Facebook, industry review platforms, marketplace profiles.
- Review themes, response quality, recency, velocity, rating distribution, competitor deltas.

## Links and Authority

- Ahrefs, Semrush, Moz, Majestic, Google Search Console when available.
- Manual checks for local media, directories, associations, sponsorships, podcasts, partners, vendors, and unlinked mentions.

## LLM Visibility

- Google AI Mode/AI Overviews when available.
- ChatGPT search/browse when available.
- Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, Bing/Copilot, and other answer engines when available.
- Record prompt, date, platform, result, citations, competitors, and caveats.

## Account Access

If the user has account access, request exports/screenshots rather than credentials:

- Google Business Profile insights.
- Google Search Console.
- Google Analytics.
- Semrush/Ahrefs/Moz exports.
- Call tracking or CRM conversion data.
