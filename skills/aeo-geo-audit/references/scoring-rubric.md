# Scoring Rubric

Score each category from 0-5.

0 = absent or actively harmful  
1 = weak, inconsistent, or mostly missing  
2 = present but shallow or unreliable  
3 = acceptable foundation with clear gaps  
4 = strong and competitive  
5 = best-in-market or clearly citation-ready

## Categories

Technical crawlability and indexability:

- 0-1: blocked, broken, non-indexable, important content hidden.
- 2-3: crawlable but with duplicate, thin, slow, or inconsistent pages.
- 4-5: clean architecture, strong internal links, accessible HTML, reliable sitemaps/canonicals.

Schema and entity clarity:

- 0-1: no usable schema, conflicting identity, inconsistent NAP.
- 2-3: basic schema but incomplete or inconsistent.
- 4-5: validated schema, clear entity IDs, accurate sameAs, service/location coverage.

AEO/GEO answer readiness:

- 0-1: vague marketing copy, no direct answers.
- 2-3: some useful answers but gaps in costs, process, comparisons, proof, or local detail.
- 4-5: direct, structured, evidence-backed answers across buyer questions.

Local/Maps readiness:

- 0-1: weak/missing GBP or location signals.
- 2-3: basic GBP/NAP presence with inconsistent categories, photos, services, or local pages.
- 4-5: strong GBP, citations, reviews, local proof, location/service-area coverage.

NAP and citations:

- 0-1: conflicting or missing name/address/phone.
- 2-3: mostly consistent but missing key citations or schema alignment.
- 4-5: clean canonical NAP and broad, accurate citation footprint.

Reputation and reviews:

- 0-1: few reviews, poor ratings, no responses, unresolved trust issues.
- 2-3: acceptable reviews but weak velocity, themes, or response quality.
- 4-5: strong ratings/count/velocity, rich sentiment, active owner responses, proof on site.

Authority, links, and mentions:

- 0-1: almost no third-party validation or spam-heavy links.
- 2-3: some citations/links but weak topical or local authority.
- 4-5: diverse, relevant, authoritative mentions and linkable assets.

LLM visibility:

- 0-1: absent or confused in sampled AI answers.
- 2-3: appears in brand prompts but not category/local prompts.
- 4-5: regularly mentioned or cited for relevant prompts with accurate facts.

Conversion trust:

- 0-1: hard to contact, no proof, unclear offering.
- 2-3: basic contact and proof but friction remains.
- 4-5: clear offer, strong proof, easy next step, transparent policies.

## Overall Readiness

- 0-20: Not AI-search ready
- 21-32: Weak foundation
- 33-40: Usable foundation, significant upside
- 41-48: Competitive
- 49-55: Strong AI/local/entity presence
