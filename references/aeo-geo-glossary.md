# AEO/GEO Glossary

## AEO

Answer Engine Optimization. The practice of making a business, page, or source easier for answer systems to understand, trust, and quote.

## GEO

Generative Engine Optimization. The practice of improving visibility, accuracy, and citation likelihood in generative AI search and answer experiences.

## Entity Clarity

How clearly systems can identify the business, its locations, services, people, profiles, and third-party references.

## NAP

Name, address, and phone. Local search systems use NAP consistency to reduce ambiguity and validate business identity.

## Citation-Worthy Content

Content that is specific, factual, structured, current, and backed by evidence, making it more likely to be used or cited by AI and search systems.
