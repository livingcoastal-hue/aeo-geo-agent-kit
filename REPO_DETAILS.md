# Repository Details

## Purpose

`aeo-geo-agent-kit` is a portable agent and skill bundle for auditing how ready a business is for AI-first discovery.

It focuses on:

- Answer Engine Optimization
- Generative Engine Optimization
- Google Maps and local search
- Entity and schema clarity
- NAP and citation consistency
- Reputation and review signals
- Authority, links, mentions, and third-party validation
- LLM visibility and citation likelihood

## Design Goals

- Work in both Codex and Claude-style skill environments.
- Produce evidence-backed audits rather than generic SEO advice.
- Separate observed facts from inferred risks.
- Turn audits into agent-ready remediation tasks.
- Keep platform-specific claims date-aware because AI search behavior changes quickly.

## Non-Goals

- Black-hat SEO
- Fake review generation
- Spam citation/link building
- Guaranteed ranking claims
- Unsourced claims about private ranking systems
